About

I work on the theoretical analysis of stochastic algorithms for sampling and optimization. My current research focuses on high-resolution Langevin dynamics, convergence in total variation, discretization error, and dimension-dependent complexity bounds.

This page collects my research interests, selected publications, projects, notes, and contact information.

Research Interests

  • Langevin-type Sampling Algorithms

    Underdamped Langevin dynamics, high-resolution dynamics, and accelerated samplers.

  • Non-asymptotic Error Analysis

    KL divergence, total variation distance, Wasserstein distance, and discretization error.

  • Hypocoercivity and Functional Inequalities

    Poincaré inequality, log-Sobolev inequality, and convergence of kinetic systems.
